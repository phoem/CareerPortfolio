## About the Team and Role

The Partnership Engineering team owns the engineering surface area required to make external partnerships and integrations successful from early Strategic Partnerships discussions through GA launch. The team provides technical validation, builds shared integration infrastructure, and enables scalable, repeatable integrations through documentation, tooling, and direct partner engagement.

The team sits at the intersection of product, platform, and external partners, working across new platform systems, Developer Platform capabilities, browser integrations, and SDK surfaces to ensure integrations are secure, extensible, and built to last.

As a Senior Developer on Partnership Engineering, you will design and build the integration patterns, components, and tooling that power high-impact external partnerships. You will work directly with partners during active integrations, contribute to shared infrastructure, and help turn one-off deals into reusable capabilities.

This is a senior individual-contributor role with broad scope. You will operate independently, shape technical direction within integrations, and influence platform evolution based on real-world partner needs.
